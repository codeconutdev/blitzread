Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start speed reading?

Paste or type any text into the text box on the home screen, then tap Start Reading. ZapRead will display one word at a time at your chosen speed.

How do I load sample text?

Tap the Load sample text button on the home screen to pre-fill a sample article so you can try ZapRead immediately.

What is ORP (Optimal Recognition Point) highlighting?

ZapRead highlights a key letter in red for each word — your eye's natural focus point. This technique, based on Spritz-style reading, helps your brain process words faster with less eye movement.

How do I change the reading speed?

On the reading screen, use the WPM (words per minute) slider at the bottom to adjust speed. Drag right to go faster, left to slow down.

Can I pause and resume reading?

Yes — tap the play/pause button on the reading screen at any time. You can also tap the rewind button to jump back 10 seconds.

Is my text stored or shared?

No. All text you paste into ZapRead stays entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your content.

Does ZapRead work offline?

Yes — ZapRead works fully offline. No internet connection is required to use the app.

What devices does ZapRead support?

ZapRead supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 16 or later.
